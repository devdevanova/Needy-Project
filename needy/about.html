Needy

Abstract

Needy is an innovative online platform designed to facilitate the seamless exchange of goods and services between individuals who have items to give and those in need. Our platform acts as a virtual bridge, connecting generous donors with individuals or families experiencing various forms of hardship. Through this digital hub, we aim to create a vibrant community of sharing, compassion, and support.

This application will be able to show the available items and contact details of their owners within a particular area.
